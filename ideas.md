# Wedding Website Design Brainstorm - Abhishek & Tejakshi

## Context
- Wedding Date: May 29, 2026
- Events: Muhurtham (5:30 AM - 7:30 AM) and Reception (6:00 PM onwards)
- Audience: Family, friends, and guests
- Tone: Elegant, romantic, celebratory

---

## Design Approach 1: Modern Minimalist Elegance
**Design Movement:** Contemporary Minimalism with Romantic Accents

**Core Principles:**
1. Clean, spacious layouts with generous whitespace
2. Subtle luxury through refined typography and delicate details
3. Emphasis on readability and content hierarchy
4. Restrained color palette with strategic accent colors

**Color Philosophy:**
- Primary: Warm cream/ivory (#FBF8F3) - timeless, sophisticated
- Accent: Deep rose gold (#C4886B) - romantic without being overpowering
- Secondary: Soft sage green (#A8B8A8) - natural, calming
- Text: Charcoal (#2C2C2C) - high contrast, readable
- Reasoning: Evokes luxury hotel aesthetics with warm, inviting tones

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric grid with left-aligned text and right-aligned imagery
- Full-width hero section with subtle parallax
- Alternating content blocks (text left/image right, then reverse)
- Floating elements and offset spacing for visual interest

**Signature Elements:**
1. Delicate gold line dividers between sections
2. Subtle floral corner accents (hand-drawn style)
3. Elegant serif typography for headings, sans-serif for body

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Smooth scroll animations revealing content
- Hover effects on event cards with gentle lift and shadow
- Fade-in transitions on page load
- Subtle micro-interactions (button scale on hover)

**Animation:**
- Fade-in on scroll for each section (200ms duration)
- Gentle scale (1.02x) on hover for interactive elements
- Smooth color transitions (300ms) on state changes
- Parallax effect on hero background at 0.3x scroll speed

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (serif) - elegant, timeless
- Heading: Playfair Display Medium - for section titles
- Body: Lato (sans-serif) - clean, highly readable
- Accent: Playfair Display italic - for quotes or special text

**Probability:** 0.08

## Selected Design Approach: Modern Minimalist Elegance

I've chosen **Modern Minimalist Elegance** as the design philosophy for this wedding website. This approach balances sophistication with accessibility, creating an elegant, timeless aesthetic that feels premium without being overly ornate.

### Why This Choice:
- **Versatility**: Works beautifully for both the early morning Muhurtham ceremony and the evening Reception
- **Readability**: Clean layout ensures event details are immediately clear to guests
- **Timelessness**: Won't feel dated in years to come
- **Emotional Resonance**: Warm cream and rose gold tones evoke romance and celebration
- **Modern Appeal**: Minimalist approach feels contemporary while maintaining elegance

### Design Philosophy Applied:
- **Warm Cream & Rose Gold**: Creates an inviting, luxurious atmosphere
- **Asymmetric Layouts**: Prevents the design from feeling static or corporate
- **Delicate Accents**: Gold dividers and subtle floral elements add romance without clutter
- **Serif + Sans-serif Pairing**: Playfair Display for elegance, Lato for clarity
- **Smooth Animations**: Subtle transitions enhance the premium feel
- **Generous Whitespace**: Allows content to breathe and feel intentional

This design will make the website feel like a carefully curated invitation—elegant, personal, and celebratory.
